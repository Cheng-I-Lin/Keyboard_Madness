Game Instructions






























Do you type fast? If not, you better learn how to do so!

In Keyboard Madness, multiple objects, or meteors, with an unique symbol written on each of them, which can be a number, a letter, or any other symbols, will start to slowly fall from the top. If one of those meteors landed on the floor, then the game ends. The objective is to type in the symbols corresponding to those on the meteors. By doing so, the corresponding meteors will be removed so that they won't collide into the floor, increasing a point for each removed meteors in the total score as well. As the score increases, the degree of difficulty of the game also increases as the speed of meteors, the amount of meteors, and the number of symbols in each meteor all increase over time. Fortunately, you may use the gadgets earned from removing meteors to help you score more points by simply clicking on the Inventory icon and select the necessary gadgets to use. It may seem easy, but once the game started, you would understand the true madness of the game. Good luck typing!

Here is a list of the different types of meteors, each with an unique characteristic and an unique method of termination, that will show up in the game.

Regular Meteor

Do not worry, this is the most basic, fundamental form of the meteor, with a range of 1 to 5 symbols on each meteor. To terminate them, simply press the keys that correspond to their symbols(no particular order is needed whn inputing the keys).

Sequential Meteor

Don't get fooled by these meteors. Although they function the same as a Regular Meteor, they need to terminated in the sequence their symbols come in(eg. if the meteor contains "A,B,C", you need to click the keys "A", "B", "C" in this particular order to terminate the meteor)

Priority Meteor

These meteors have unsually large gravitational pull, meaning that it attracts all the symbols you entered. To terminate other meteors, you would need to terminate these Priority Meteors first.

Godspeed Meteor

Watch out! Look very closely for this one. This meteor travels at a great speed, crushing down faster than all other meteors. Although it's fast, it only contains one symbol, so you just need to be ready when it comes.

Colossal Meteor

Opposite to a Godspeed Meteor, a Colossal Meteor is a slow moving, massive meteor. Although it is slower than all other meteors, it's incredible mass requires you to input the corresponding symbol 100 TIMES to terminate it!

Super Meteor

Similar to a Regular Meteor, a Super Meteor only has 1 to 5 symbols. However, it will not be affected by the power of any gadgets, meaning that it can't be slowed down, terminated, and etc using gadgets.

Hidden Meteor

As you might have guessed it a Hidden Meteor has parts of its symbols hidden, revealing only the first symbol. To terminate it, you need to go in order like how you would against a Sequential Meteor. Removing the first symbol would reveal the second symbol, allowing you to remove the second symbol which thus reveals the third symbol, and so on and so on until all symbols are terminated.

Meteor Shower

Although it might only have 1 to 5 symbols, when terminated, it will produce two more Regular Meteors at the exact spot where it was terminated, so you better act fast to these meteors.

Left Shift Meteor

As the name suggested, these meteors require you to hold the "shift" key at the LEFT side of the keyboard when entering the other keys corresponding to the symbols of the meteors. Only by doing so can you terminate the meteors.

Right Shift Meteor

As the name suggested, these meteors require you to hold the "shift" key at the RIGHT side of the keyboard when entering the other keys corresponding to the symbols of the meteors. Only by doing so can you terminate the meteors.

Gadgets Category






























Lower Elevation
Decreases the floor height, allowing the meteors to fall through the sky for a longer distance. Therefore, there is more time for you to terminate them by clicking the right keys.

Magical Eraser
As simple as one tap, all meteors would be cleared and terminated. MAGIC!

Time Quiescence
By stopping the flow of time, all meteor movements cease to occur, allowing you to terminate them with ease.
Meteor Reduction
"Wow, there's too much meteors for me to handle!" you might say. Don't worry, this gadget helps you decrease the time of meteors generation.
Immunity
With this gadget activated, preventive measures are taken to protect you from the crushing meteor. Although it can only be use one at a time, it's still a life saver!
Lucky Charm
You indeed are a lucky star! When this gadget is activated each time, the probability of terminating one meteor randomly increases. Now that's some luck right there.
Hot Streak
You are on FIRE right now! Each time when a meteor is terminated double the points are added in a duration of 10 seconds.

Juggernaut
Need some help? This juggernaut will surely help you by removing all meteors that come in contact with it.
Omnipotent
With the power of the gods, you now will have unlimited power for 5 seconds, allowing you to terminate every meteor no matter which key you pressed.
All-In Gambit
Go big or go home! Remove all your items in your current inventory in return of gaining 5 points per item removed. That's straight cash!
Equal Trade
Forming a balanced, equal trade requires serious thinking. Would you be willing to trade for one item in
exchange for an increase in the floor height?
Shuffle
You might not like your hands, huh? That's okay because you can use this gadget to refresh all items in your inventory to new ones.
Time Rewind
Not fast enough to terminate all meteors? Solve this problem by simply pushing them backward, rewinding time itself to give you more time.
Primitive Meteors
Some meteors are too overpowered in terms of their levels, so use this gadget to turn them to the standard,
regular form!
Digits Only
Maybe there are too much buttons and keys for you to click. How about just forget about other keys and use only the number keys?
Letters Only
Maybe there are too much buttons and keys for you to click. How about just forget about other keys and use only the alphabet keys?
Symbols Only
Maybe there are too much buttons and keys for you to click. How about just forget about other keys and use only the symbols keys?
Arrows Only
Maybe there are too much buttons and keys for you to click. How about just forget about other keys and use only the arrows keys?
Restricted Area
Danger! Danger! You may not enter this zone! Any meteor that enters this randomly generated area will be terminated.
Infinity Barrier
Slowing down all meteor movements as they get closer to the floor, just like a barrier.
