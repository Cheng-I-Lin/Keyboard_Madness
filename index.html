Game Instructions






























Do you type fast? If not, you better learn how to do so!

In Keyboard Madness, multiple objects, or meteors, with an unique symbol written on each of them, which can be a number, a letter, or any other symbols, will start to slowly fall from the top. If one of those meteors landed on the floor, then the game ends. The objective is to type in the symbols corresponding to those on the meteors. By doing so, the corresponding meteors will be removed so that they won't collide into the floor, increasing a point for each removed meteors in the total score as well. As the score increases, the degree of difficulty of the game also increases as the speed of meteors, the amount of meteors, and the number of symbols in each meteor all increase over time. Fortunately, you may use the gadgets earned from removing meteors to help you score more points by simply clicking on the Inventory icon and select the necessary gadgets to use. It may seem easy, but once the game started, you would understand the true madness of the game. Good luck typing!
